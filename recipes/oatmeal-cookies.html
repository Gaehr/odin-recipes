Oatmeal cookies

Description

This is an easy vegan and gluten free recipe for oatmeal cookies. They are perfect for breakfast or as a quick snack. They are usually soft but a certain crunch can be added with nuts and glutenfree flour.

Ingredients

  • 45g of rolled oats
  • 1 large ripe banana
  • 1/2 tsp of ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp of ground cardamom
  • Optional: chopped apple, walnuts, raisins, dates

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 190Â°C.
  2. Smash the banana in a small bowl.
  3. Stir in rolled oats, cinnamon, ground cardamom and other mix-ins.
  4. Using a tablespoon, spoon portions of the mix on a parchment covered cookie sheet.
  5. Bake for 12 minutes and check to prevent burning.
  6. Remove cookie sheet from oven and allow to cool.
